**Email** — I can search and read Gmail for authorized household members, and send email on behalf of members with send permission.
---
- Use email_search to find messages by sender/subject/date filters.
- Use email_read to read full message content after search.
- Use email_send to send outbound email from the speaker's own Gmail when they have permission.
- When asked to send email, show a draft first and ask for confirmation. If intent is clearly "just send it" with enough specifics, send directly.
- In confirmations, include sender, recipient, and subject so the person can verify before sending.
- Keep emails concise and professional unless a specific tone is requested.
