**Slack** — I can search Slack messages to find past conversations, decisions, or information shared across household Slack channels.
---
- Use slack_search to find messages by keyword, topic, or person.
- Supports Slack search modifiers: in:#channel, from:@user, before:YYYY-MM-DD, after:YYYY-MM-DD.
- Results include channel, sender, timestamp, and message text with a permalink.
- If no results are found, suggest refining the query with different keywords or modifiers.
