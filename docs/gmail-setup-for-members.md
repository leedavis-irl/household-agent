# Gmail Setup for Household Members

## What This Does
Connects your Gmail to Iji so you can ask things like:
- "Do I have any unread emails?"
- "Search my email for the school newsletter"
- "Read the last email from my doctor's office"

Iji gets **read-only** access — it can search and read your email but cannot send, delete, or modify anything.

## What You Need
- 2 minutes at Lee's computer (or screen-sharing with Lee)
- Be signed into your Google account in a browser

## Steps

1. **Lee starts the setup** — she runs a script on her computer that generates a personalized link for you.

2. **Open the link** — Lee will share the link with you or open it on her computer. Sign into your Google account if prompted.

3. **Click through the warning** — You'll see "Google hasn't verified this app." This is normal for personal/household apps.
   - Click **Advanced**
   - Click **Go to Iji Household Agent (unsafe)** — it's not actually unsafe, Google just says this for unverified apps

4. **Grant access** — Click **Allow** to give Iji read-only access to your Gmail.

5. **Done!** — The page will say "Authorization successful." You can close the browser tab.

## After Setup
Ask Iji anything about your email via Signal:
- "Search my email for Amazon receipts from this month"
- "Any new emails from the school?"
- "Read my latest email from [person]"

## Privacy
- Iji can only **read** your email — no sending, editing, or deleting
- Only household members with the right permissions can search your email
- Lee (as admin) can search anyone's email; other adults can only search their own
- You can revoke access anytime at https://myaccount.google.com/permissions

## Questions?
Ask Lee, or message Iji: "Can you read my email?" — it'll tell you if it's set up or not.
